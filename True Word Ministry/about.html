About Us

At True Word Ministry our is to help believers in the good news of Jesus Christ fulfill their purpose and be overcomers. Ultimately for us it comes down to spreading the gospel, and our way of doing that is by ordaining ministers to aid them on their journey, and by offering classes on prophetic teaching and leadership and youth and marriage counseling and more. The board of directors of True Word Ministry believe that the Lord inspires us all from within and that God did not prescribe any schooling or course of study to be a requirement to preach and pastor. If the Lord has spoken to you and it is your calling to preach, do a prison ministry, or just marry a friend or family member then it is our mission to enable you to achieve your calling.
